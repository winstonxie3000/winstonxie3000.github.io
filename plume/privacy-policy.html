Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 June 2026 · App: Plume (package app.plume.notes)

The short version: Plume collects nothing. Your notes live only on your device. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, no trackers, and the app makes no network calls at runtime. The only way data leaves your device is when you choose to export it.

1. Information we collect

None. Plume does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. We have no servers and no backend. Plume does not ask for your name, email, phone number, location, or contacts, and contains no advertising or analytics SDKs.

2. Where your notes are stored

Everything you create in Plume — notes, checklists, tags, colors, reminders, and settings — is stored locally on your device only (in the app's private on-device storage). It is never uploaded to us or to any third party. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, that information is permanently removed from your device.

3. Device permissions

  • Notifications — used only to deliver the optional note reminders you set yourself. Reminders are scheduled locally on your device; no reminder content is sent anywhere.
  • Billing — used only if you choose to buy the optional one-time Plume Pro unlock. The purchase is processed by Google Play; Plume never sees or stores your payment details. We only store a local flag indicating that Pro is unlocked.

4. Purchases

Plume offers an optional one-time in-app purchase (“Plume Pro”). Payments are handled entirely by Google Play Billing under Google's terms and privacy policy. We do not receive your financial information.

5. Data sharing & third parties

We do not share data with anyone because we do not collect any. The only third party involved is Google Play, and only when you make a purchase or install/update the app. See the Google Privacy Policy.

6. Your control over your data

You are always in full control. You can export all of your notes to a file you own at any time, import them onto another device, and delete everything by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

7. Children's privacy

Plume is safe for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
